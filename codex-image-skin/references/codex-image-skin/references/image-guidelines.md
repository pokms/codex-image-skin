# Image Guidelines

Use a local PNG, JPEG, or BMP that you are allowed to use.

- Prefer a landscape image at least 1280 pixels wide. The hero is wide and crops with `cover` positioning.
- Keep a calmer or darker region on the left when possible. Codex keeps real native text and controls there.
- Keep important subjects near the right or center-right. The default crop uses `right center`.
- Avoid using a screenshot of an entire Codex window. The generated image is a hero asset, not a replacement UI.
- Source images, converted `theme-reference.png`, generated palette files, and backups are local user artifacts. Keep them out of commits.
